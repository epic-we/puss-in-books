This game was developed in collaboration with young game jammers as part of the EPIC-WE project.

EPIC-WE is an EU-funded initiative that brings together young creatives, cultural institutions, and game industry professionals to explore and express cultural values with game-making.

Through a series of game jams, participants designed and developed unique experiences reflecting their perspectives on heritage, society, and the future.

For additional information, visit: https://epic-we.eu
